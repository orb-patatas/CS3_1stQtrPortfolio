My Favorite Recipe

I luv Leche Flan because it’s creamy, sweet, and always present at our family celebrations.


Ingredients

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can (300 ml) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (370 ml) evaporated milk
  • 1 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Melt the sugar in a pan over low heat until it turns into golden caramel. Pour into a llanera or baking dish and spread evenly.
  2. In a bowl, mix egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract. Stir gently to avoid bubbles.
  3. Pour the mixture into the caramel-coated llanera.
  4. Cover with foil and steam or bake using a water bath for about 45–60 minutes, or until set.
  5. Let it cool, then refrigerate before serving.
  6. Invert onto a plate to release the caramel on top.
